About DNC Trucking, LLC

DNC Trucking, LLC is a family-oriented business based out of Minneapolis, MN. We are locally owned and operated. Our typical delivery area is the 7-county metro area of Minneapolis. We also deliver to the Brainerd, Alexandria, Duluth areas, and anywhere in MN! We would like to be your new delivery service.

What We Do

We specialize in job site delivery/trucking of building materials such as roofing, siding, metal roofing, flat roofing, and small forgotten materials. We have the capability to unload your products on-site with our truck-mounted forklifts, which can lift 5,000 lbs. and deliver in tight spots.

Our fleet also includes Ram Promaster vans for delivering packages, small orders, and materials that need protection from the elements.
